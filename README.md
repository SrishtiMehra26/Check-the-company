# Company Check - AI-Powered Company Research Platform

## Overview
**Company Check** is an interactive web application that helps you research companies quickly and efficiently. Search for any company with a Wikipedia page worldwide and get comprehensive information including company details, employee ratings, locations, and interview preparation materials.

## Features

### 🔍 Company Search
- Search any company registered worldwide
- Access companies of all sizes, from global corporations to small local businesses
- Real-time information gathering from the web

### 📊 Company Information
- **Company Profile**: What the company does and their core business
- **Company Logo/Image**: Visual representation from company sources
- **Kununu Ratings**: Employee satisfaction ratings with star visualization
- **Strongest Areas**: Key expertise, specializations, and focus areas
- **Locations**: Headquarters and major office locations worldwide

### 💼 Interview Preparation
- **Customized Interview Questions**: Questions tailored specifically to each company
- **Suggested Answers**: Professional response examples for interview success
- **Company-Specific Insights**: Information relevant to the company's industry and values

### 🎮 Interactive Game
- Company-specific games that relate to what the company does
- Fun way to learn more about the company's industry

### ℹ️ Additional Information
- Company achievements and recent developments
- Key facts and milestones
- Industry-relevant insights

## How to Use

1. **Open the website** in your web browser
2. **Search for a company** using the search bar at the top
3. **View company information** in the four-quadrant layout:
   - Left side: Company image, profile, and interview questions
   - Right side: Ratings, strongest areas, and locations
4. **Scroll down** to see suggested interview answers and play the interactive game
5. **Learn more** with additional company information

## Website Layout

The website is divided into 4 quadrants:

- **Quadrant 1 (Top Right)**: Kununu ratings, strongest areas, locations
- **Quadrant 2 (Top Left)**: Company image, short profile
- **Quadrant 3 (Bottom Left)**: Interview questions customized for the company
- **Quadrant 4 (Bottom Right)**: Interview answers and interactive game
