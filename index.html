CompanySearch

Explore curated Top 100 Worldwide and Top 100 Germany companies — friendly, fast, and interactive.

Quick start — it's easy:
Choose which list you want at the top-right (Worldwide or Germany). Type a company name in the search box — the app searches the entire selected Top 100. For a quick browse, click one of the tiles (popular picks). When you open a company the profile expands with a concise summary, official link, practical interview tips, and a short quiz — all designed to help you learn about the company fast.
