CompanySearch

Explore curated Top 100 Worldwide and Top 100 Germany companies — friendly, fast, and interactive.

Quick start — it’s easy:
Choose which list you want (Worldwide or Germany) on the top-right. Type a company name in the search box and press Search or Enter — the app searches the entire selected Top 100. Browse the popular tiles for quick picks. Click any company to open a larger profile with a concise summary, official link, tailored interview tips, and a short quiz to test what you learned.
Client-only, no backend
